Our Recruitment Process

From search to
start date.

A five-stage process built around quality, transparency, and a result you can rely on — for both candidates and client companies.

I Intake & Discovery

We learn your technical requirements, team culture, and the non-negotiables that make someone the right fit — not just the right CV.

II Active Sourcing

Beyond job boards — we reach into our own talent network, alumni community, and referral channels to surface candidates others never see.

III Deep Screening

Technical assessments, cultural alignment interviews, and thorough reference verification. Only those who pass every stage reach your desk.

IV Curated Presentation

We present 2–3 thoroughly vetted candidates with detailed profiles, not a shortlist of 20. Every introduction comes with our full conviction.

V Offer & Onboard

We facilitate negotiations, manage the offer process, and support both sides through day one and beyond. The relationship lasts long after the placement.

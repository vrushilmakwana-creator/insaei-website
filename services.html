How We Engage

Three hiring models.
One commitment.

Different situations call for different engagement types. Whether you're hiring for a six-month project, bringing on a long-term employee, or exploring a temp-to-perm path — we support them all.

W2
Contract (W2)
Short-term projects, fixed duration

For time-bound needs or temporary capacity gaps. We place professionals as W2 contractors — delivering skill without long-term commitment.

C2H
Contract to Hire
Start temp, convert to full-time

The try-before-you-buy model. A candidate joins your team on contract first, with a conversion option built in from day one.

1099
1099 Contract
Independent Contractor Engagement

We place skilled IT professionals on 1099 contracts for project-based or hourly engagements. Candidates operate as independent contractors, managing their own taxes and benefits, while companies gain flexibility, faster hiring cycles, and access to specialized talent without long-term payroll commitments.

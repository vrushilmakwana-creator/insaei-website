What We Stand For

Four values.
One commitment.

Every decision at innsæi — from how we market your profile to how we prepare you for interviews — is guided by these four principles.

I
Precision Over Volume

We don't blanket job boards with your résumé. We target roles that match your skills, level, and aspirations — then pursue them relentlessly until the right door opens.

II
Daily Accountability

You'll hear from us every day. Not just when there's good news — but when we're pushing applications, scheduling calls, and updating strategy. Silence is not part of our process.

III
Candidate First

Your career goals come first. Every recommendation we make — every role we pitch — is evaluated from your perspective. We work on behalf of you, always.

IV
End-to-End Partnership

From the first profile review to your first day on the job — and beyond. Background checks, onboarding paperwork, 30-day check-ins: we stay engaged through all of it.

How We Work

A process built
around you.

Every candidate gets a dedicated process — not a shared queue. Here's what working with innsæi looks like.

I
Free Consultation & Profile Audit

We understand your skills, goals, and target roles — then audit your profile to identify exactly what needs to change before we go to market.

II
Profile Optimisation & Launch

We rewrite and optimise your résumé and online profiles to U.S. hiring standards — ATS-friendly, keyword-rich, and positioned for your target roles.

III
Daily Active Marketing

Your profile goes live across 20+ platforms. We apply, outreach, and follow up every single day — reporting progress back to you constantly.

IV
Interview Preparation & Support

Mock sessions, technical prep, and company research before every interview. You walk in confident because we've prepared every angle.

V
Offer, Onboarding & Beyond

From salary negotiation to background checks to day one — we're there. And we check in after you start to make sure everything is going well.

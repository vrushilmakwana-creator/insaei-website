The Marketing Process

How we market
your profile.

Our marketing process is not a one-time submission — it is a continuous, daily effort across multiple platforms and channels designed to get your profile in front of the right decision-makers.

01
Profile Optimisation

Craft a profile that
demands attention.

Before a single application is submitted, our team audits and rewrites your resume and professional profiles to align with what top U.S. employers actively look for. We highlight the right skills, reframe your experience, and use industry-specific language that gets you past applicant tracking systems (ATS) and into human hands.

02
Multi-Platform Distribution

20+ portals,
one daily push.

Our marketing team submits and actively promotes your profile across 20+ major job portals and recruitment channels every single day. Platforms include LinkedIn, Indeed, Dice, Monster, ZipRecruiter, CareerBuilder, and direct outreach to hiring managers at target companies. We don't wait for opportunities — we create them.

✦ We stay connected with candidates daily throughout the entire process — proactively updating strategy and reporting progress.

03
Targeted Outreach

Reaching the right
people directly.

Beyond portals, our team conducts targeted outreach to recruiters, talent acquisition teams, and hiring managers at companies aligned with your profile. We research, identify, and contact decision-makers directly — bypassing the noise and getting your profile seen by the people who matter.

04
Interview & Conversion

From interview call
to offer letter.

When interviews are secured, our team provides dedicated preparation support: company research, role-specific mock sessions, technical drills, and communication coaching. We ensure you walk in confident and walk out with an offer. Our conversion rate from interview to offer is consistently above industry benchmarks.

05
Post-Placement Support

We stay until
you're settled.

Our relationship doesn't end at the offer letter. We support you through background verification, onboarding paperwork, and your first 90 days in role. Smooth transitions lead to long-term success — and long-term success is how we measure our own performance.
